Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical repair take?

Most repairs are completed within 24-48 hours. Simple issues like screen replacement or battery changes can be done the same day, while complex motherboard repairs may take 2-3 days.

Do you provide warranty on repairs?

Yes, all our repairs come with a warranty. The warranty period varies depending on the type of repair and parts used. We'll inform you about the warranty details before starting the repair.

Can I get a quote before repair?

Absolutely! We provide free diagnosis and transparent pricing before starting any repair. You'll know exactly what the repair will cost with no hidden charges.

Do you work on all brands?

Yes, we repair all major brands including Apple, Dell, HP, Lenovo, Acer, Asus, Oppo, Vivo, and many more. Our technicians are trained on various brands and models.

Is my data safe during repair?

We take data security very seriously. In most cases, your data remains safe during repairs. However, we always recommend backing up your important data before any repair service.

Do you offer pickup and delivery?

Currently, we offer repair services at our shop location. However, we can arrange for pickup and delivery for bulk orders or corporate clients. Contact us for more details.
